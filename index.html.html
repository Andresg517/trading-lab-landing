Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need any experience to join?
Absolutely not. The Trading Lab is built from the ground up for complete beginners. We start with the very basics of how markets work and build up from there. If you can follow instructions, you can learn to trade.
How much capital do I need to start?
Zero. You can paper trade (simulated trading with fake money) completely for free while you learn. When you're ready to go live, we recommend starting with $500–$2,000 depending on your risk tolerance.
Is this really lifetime access?
Yes. After completing your 3 monthly payments of $999 (or $799 with code LAUNCH), you keep full access to every lesson, future updates, and the community forever. No hidden renewals.
Do you provide trading signals?
We share real-time setups in the live chat, but our goal is to teach you to find your own trades — not to depend on signals. The course teaches you the exact framework we use so you can be fully independent.
What markets does this cover?
The strategies and technical analysis in the course apply to stocks, futures, and crypto. The core concepts are universal across markets, so you can trade wherever you're most comfortable.
